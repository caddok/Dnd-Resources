PRINTING INSTRUCTIONS

1. Combine desired spells into a single document with an online PDF combiner.

2. Open combined PDF in Acrobat Reader.

3. In the print menu, select Multiple under Page Sizing & Handling

4. Select 4 by 2 pages per sheet and landscape orientation
