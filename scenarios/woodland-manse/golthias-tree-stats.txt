Gulthias Tree encounter
AC: 15
HP: 200-250
Current HP: 200-250
Speed: 0 (immovable)
Proficiency + 2

Condition Immunities -> blinded, deafened

STR	DEX	CON	INT	WIS	CHA
20  0	20   6	10   3 

Actions:

Spawn Defenders(Recharge 4-6).
The Gulthias tree can spawn blights to defends itself. Roll a d20. On an even
number the tree makes 1d6 Needle Blights in his crown. On an odd number the tree
makes 1d4 Vine Blights


Legendary Actions:
The Gulthias tree has a number of legendary actions equal to the number of players - 1

Grappling Branch (1 action):
Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 15 ft., one creature not grappled by the tree. 
The target is grappled (escape DC 15) and restrained. Until the grapple ends, the target 
takes 9 (2d8) necrotic damage at the start of each of its turns. While the target is grappled
any damage dealt to it will recover the same amount to the Gulthias tree
The root has AC 15 and can be severed by dealing 7 slashing damage at once. Cutting the branch 
hurts the blight and ends the grapple when destroyed.
